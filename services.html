Commercial Plans

Private Usage (of Stealth and Radar) is free for everyone, but has a ratio of Beacons and Echoes that requires users to contribute to the public Radar of Tholian Network.

That ratio depends on the health status of the Network. Currently, the minimum ratio is around 1/10 that need to be contributed by free users in order to continue access to the public Radar's Knowledge.

Commercial Plans allow users to use Beacons and Echoes from the public Radar of Tholian Network with a higher volume than are allowed for Private users.

After the inclusive volume in the plan is surpassed, the minimum ratio of required contributions applies.

Private

  • No Commercial Usage allowed
  • 100 included Beacons or Echoes / month

Lieutenant

  • Commercial Usage allowed
  • 1000 included Beacons or Echoes / month
  • Trusted Peers for Sharing
  • Peer-to-Peer Encryption
  • Export to CSV file

Commander

  • Commercial Usage allowed
  • 10000 included Beacons or Echoes / month
  • Trusted Peers for Sharing
  • Peer-to-Peer Encryption
  • Export to CSV file
  • Export to SQL database

Admiral

  • Commercial Usage allowed
  • Unlimited Beacons or Echoes / month
  • Trusted Peers for Sharing
  • Peer-to-Peer Encryption
  • Export to CSV file
  • Export to SQL database
